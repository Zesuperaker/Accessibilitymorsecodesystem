Vision & Purpose

Inspired by witnessing the struggle of a classmate with Rett Syndrome, Iris was developed to dismantle the cost and hardware barriers of modern assistive technology.

By leveraging the smartphones and tablets already in use globally, we provide a compact, clinical-grade solution for those with motor impairments—turning a simple blink into a powerful voice.

How It Works

1

Visual Capture

Iris maps 68 facial landmarks in real-time using your existing camera. No specialized sensors or expensive hardware required.

2

Neural Processing

Our engine distinguishes between natural involuntary movement and intentional long/short triggers to form Morse patterns.

3

Dynamic Output

Patterns are decoded into text, natural speech, or system navigation, enabling total digital independence.

Core Capabilities

Smart Morse Engine

Adaptive technology that learns your blinking cadence to maximize Words Per Minute (WPM).

Multimodal Communication

Integrated Text-to-Voice (TTS) and system-wide commands provide a diverse range of digital interaction.

Local Edge Processing

All biometric data stays on your device. We prioritize privacy by processing everything in-browser.

Global Accessibility

Compatible with any camera-enabled device, significantly reducing the cost of assistive tools.
